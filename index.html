    Cooking Training Guide

    Shrimp & Anchovies

    Level 1-15

    Fish and cook at Lumbridge or Al Kharid. ~30K XP/hr. Great for brand new accounts.

    Trout & Salmon

    Level 15-30

    Fish at Barbarian Village, cook on the nearby fire/range. ~50K XP/hr.

    Tuna & Lobsters

    Level 30-50

    Buy raw from GE or fish at Catherby. Cook on the Catherby range (diary bonus). ~100K XP/hr.

    Wines (Jug of Wine)

    Level 35-68

    Fastest method in the game. Buy grapes + jugs of water from GE, combine. ~500K XP/hr! Cheap and AFK.

    Lobsters → Swordfish → Monkfish

    Level 50-80

    Cook on the Hosidius Kitchen range (requires 100% Hosidius favour) for lowest burn rates. ~150-200K XP/hr.

    Sharks

    Level 80-93

    Stop burning at 99 (94 with cooking gauntlets + Hosidius range). ~200K XP/hr. Small profit with gauntlets.

    Anglerfish

    Level 84-99

    Profitable at high levels! Stop burning at 99 (93 with gauntlets + Hosidius). ~150K XP/hr but good GP.

    Karambwans (1-tick)

    Level 30+ (optimal 90+)

    1-tick cooking karambwans is the fastest traditional method at ~800K XP/hr. Requires practice!

    Cooking Milestones

    Cooking Gauntlets: From the Family Crest quest. Reduces burn rates significantly — essential for profit cooking!
    Hosidius Range: 100% Hosidius favour unlocks the kitchen range with the lowest burn rates in the game.

          Wilderness Bosses

          Scorpia

          • Location: Scorpia's Cave, level 53 Wilderness
          • Pray Protect from Melee
          • Weak to crush attacks
          • At 50% HP spawns 2 Guardians (healers) — kill them ASAP
          • Bring antipoison
          • Single-combat in the cave = PKers can only 1v1

          Chaos Fanatic

          • Location: West of Lava Maze, level 42 Wilderness
          • Pray Protect from Missiles
          • Special attack unequips your gear — just re-equip
          • Multi-combat area — bring cheap gear

          Crazy Archaeologist

          • Location: South of Ruins, level 23 Wilderness
          • Pray Protect from Missiles
          • Dodge the book special (3x3 area)
          • Easiest of the three — low risk area

          Raids Quick Reference

          Chambers of Xeric (CoX)

          • Room layouts are random — need multiple runs for all music tracks
          • Soloable with high combat stats
          • Don't need to complete for music — just enter rooms
          • Key rooms: Ice Demon, Olm, Vasa, Vanguards, Tekton, Muttadiles

          Theatre of Blood (ToB)

          • Entry mode is soloable with decent gear
          • Each room has its own music track: Maiden, Bloat, Nylocas, Sotetseg, Xarpus, Verzik
          • Check in-game music list to confirm which you have

          Tombs of Amascut (ToA)

          • Solo-friendly with invocation system
          • Start at 0 invocations to learn
          • Music tracks unlock per room
